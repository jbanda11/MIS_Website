About Me

I was born in the border town of El Paso, Texas, into a hardworking and loving Mexican-American household in May of 1997. It is in El Paso, that I grew up and started to get a feel for what I want to accomplish in this world. For a long time, I thought that meant I'd be a professional basketball player. Even though that didn't exactly pan out, to this day, I credit basketball for teaching me discipline and for showing me what it's like to work hard every day for something I love. After graduating from high school in 2015, I moved to Austin to pursue a degree in computer science. Now, as my time on the UT campus comes to an end, it'll be bittersweet to say goodbye, but thrilling to see what's ahead.
